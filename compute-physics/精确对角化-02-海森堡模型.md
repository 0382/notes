H = J\sum_{i}\vec{S}_{i} \cdot \vec{S}_{i+1}

$$
H = J\sum_{i}[\frac{1}{2}(S_{i}^+S_{i+1}^- + S_i^-S_{i+1}^+) + S_{i}^zS_{i+1}^z]
